## üí° SEO Writing Tips for All Blog Posts

### Keyword Usage
- **Title**: Include primary keyword
- **First paragraph**: Use primary keyword in first 100 words
- **H2/H3**: Include variations of keywords naturally
- **Throughout**: Use LSI keywords (related terms)
- **Conclusion**: Use primary keyword once
- **Meta description**: Include primary keyword
- **URL**: Use primary keyword (separated by hyphens)

### Internal Linking
- Link to 3-5 related pages on your website
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Link to service pages, other blog posts, contact page
- Help readers navigate to relevant information

### External Linking
- Link to 2-3 authoritative sources
- Government websites (.gov)
- Industry associations
- Research studies
- Reputable news sources
- NO competitor links!

### Content Quality
- Answer user intent completely
- Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Bullet points for easy scanning
- Include examples and real data
- Write for humans, not just search engines
- Grade 8-9 reading level
- No grammatical errors

### Images
- At least 5 images per post
- Optimize file size (under 200KB)
- Descriptive filenames: nepal-cargo-truck.jpg (not IMG_1234.jpg)
- ALT text with keywords: "Capital Cargo truck delivering freight in Kathmandu Nepal"
- Add captions where helpful

### Engagement Elements
- Questions to engage readers
- Statistics and data points
- Real examples and case studies
- Customer testimonials
- Quotes from experts
- Downloadable resources (checklists, guides)

### CTAs (Calls-to-Action)
- **Top of post**: Soft CTA (download guide, watch video)
- **Middle of post**: Service-related CTA
- **End of post**: Strong conversion CTA (get quote, contact us)
- Use buttons for important CTAs
- Make CTAs specific and action-oriented

### Format for Readability
- Short paragraphs
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- Bullet points and numbered lists
- Bold important points
- Tables for comparisons
- Blockquotes for testimonials
- White space - don't crowd the page

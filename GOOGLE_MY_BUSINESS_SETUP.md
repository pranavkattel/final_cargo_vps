### Step 1: Claim Your Business

1. Go to: https://business.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account (use company email)
3. Click "Add your business to Google"
4. Enter business name: **Capital Cargo**

### Step 2: Choose Business Category

**Primary Category**: Freight Forwarding Service  
**Additional Categories**:
- Shipping Company
- Logistics Service
- Cargo Service
- Air Freight Forwarding Service
- Import Export Company

### Step 3: Add Location

```
Business Name: Capital Cargo
Street Address: [Your exact street address]
City: Kathmandu
State/Province: Bagmati Province

Country: Nepal
```

**Important**: Use your actual physical address where customers can visit or contact you.

### Step 4: Add Service Area

Since you serve multiple locations, add these service areas:
- Kathmandu
- Bhaktapur
- Lalitpur
- Pokhara
- Chitwan
- Biratnagar
- Birgunj
- All of Nepal

### Step 5: Contact Information

```
Phone Number: +977-1-XXXXXXX
Website: https://cargocapital.com
```

**Tip**: Make sure this phone number matches the one on your website!

### Step 6: Verify Your Business

Google will send verification:
- **By postcard** (5-14 days): Recommended
- **By phone** (if available)
- **By email** (if available)

Choose postcard verification and wait for the code.

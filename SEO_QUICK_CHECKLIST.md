## üö® Common SEO Mistakes to Avoid

- ‚ùå **Keyword stuffing** - Use keywords naturally (1-2% density)
- ‚ùå **Duplicate content** - Make each page unique
- ‚ùå **Slow loading speed** - Keep under 3 seconds
- ‚ùå **Not mobile-friendly** - 60% of searches are mobile
- ‚ùå **Ignoring local SEO** - GMB is critical for local business
- ‚ùå **No backlinks** - Quality backlinks are essential
- ‚ùå **Ignoring reviews** - Reviews impact local SEO heavily
- ‚ùå **Inconsistent NAP** - Same name/address/phone everywhere
- ‚ùå **No analytics** - Can't improve what you don't measure
- ‚ùå **Giving up too soon** - SEO takes 3-6 months to show results
